AgroVision Robot

The AgroVision Robot is a robotic system built on ROS and powered by the NVIDIA Jetson Nano. It autonomously navigates agricultural environments, employing
deep learning and computer vision to detect diseases on plants. At its core, the Robot is engineered to operate autonomously within diverse agricultural settings
such as fields or greenhouses. Equipped with an array of sensors and high-resolution cameras, it navigates through the terrain with precision, avoiding obstacles
and efficiently covering large areas of crops.
